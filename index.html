06 · Industries

Your Sector. Your Requirements.

Healthcare & Life Sciences

FDA AI/ML Guidance. HIPAA. ONC. CMS.

FDA AI/ML SaMD HIPAA ONC Interoperability CMS Requirements

AI in clinical decision support requires documented risk classification before deployment. Patient data processed by vendor AI constitutes covered entity risk under HIPAA. CMS and payers increasingly require AI governance evidence before contract award.

FDA AI/ML SaMD Guidance

Software as a Medical Device frameworks require documented change control and risk classification for AI-driven clinical tools.

HIPAA AI Accountability

Third-party AI vendors processing PHI extend your covered entity obligations. Most mid-market healthcare companies have unexamined exposure here.

ONC & CMS Requirements

Interoperability rules increasingly touch AI-powered data exchange. CMS contracting now includes AI governance as a procurement signal.

Our Approach

We align your AI inventory and controls to FDA AI/ML SaMD guidance and HIPAA administrative safeguard requirements, giving your compliance team audit-ready documentation your examiners will accept.

Financial Services

CFPB. OCC SR 11-7. SEC AI Disclosures.

CFPB AI Fairness OCC SR 11-7 SEC Disclosures Enforcement Actions

Credit, underwriting, and fraud models require explainability documentation for regulatory review. Model risk management extends to third-party AI — not just internal models. Regulators are actively issuing AI-specific enforcement actions.

OCC SR 11-7 Model Risk

The OCC's model risk management guidance now applies to third-party AI, not just internally developed models. Vendor AI is your risk.

CFPB AI Fairness Guidance

CFPB expects explainability for adverse action decisions made using AI. Documentation gaps are enforcement vulnerabilities.

SEC AI Disclosure Requirements

Public companies face increasing SEC scrutiny on AI use disclosure. Material AI risk must be identified, assessed, and documented.

Our Approach

We map your AI systems to SR 11-7 model risk principles and CFPB fair lending requirements, producing the documentation your examiners will ask for — before they ask for it.

Professional & B2B Services

ISO 42001 in RFPs. Enterprise Procurement. Client Confidentiality.

ISO/IEC 42001 Enterprise Procurement Client Confidentiality

Enterprise clients are adding AI governance clauses to vendor contracts as pass/fail criteria. Legal and consulting firms face confidentiality exposure from AI tools used on matters. ISO 42001 is appearing in procurement questionnaires.
